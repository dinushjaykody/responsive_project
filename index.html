Max is the life size mascot for Scouts Victoria. He is available to attend Scouting and Public events to help promote Scouting. If you wish to book Max to assist your Group, District or Region at a promotional event, please send an email to max@vicscouts.asn.au Max is managed by Team Max, which is made up of Venturers, Rovers and Leaders. If you are interested in joining Team Max as a performer or as a general team member, contact us by emailing max@vicscouts.asn.au
